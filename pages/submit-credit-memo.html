Credit Memo Request

This form is used to submit a credit memo request FCC Finance. You will receive an automated acknowledgment after your request is submitted and will receive and email when the credit memo has been approved and processed.

The list of approvers is based on the largest credit memo amount. Credit less than $5,000 may be approved by supervisor. Request between $5,000 and $25,000 require a manager approval. Requests greater than $25,000 require an officer or direct approval.
